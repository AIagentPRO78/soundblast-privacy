Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

SoundBlast is a fun soundboard app for macOS. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about how (little) data is involved.

Data Collection

SoundBlast does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely offline on your Mac.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (favourite sounds and volume level) locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

SoundBlast contains no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

Network Access

SoundBlast makes no network requests. It does not connect to the internet for any reason.

Children's Privacy

SoundBlast is safe for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, there are no concerns regarding children's privacy (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions, reach us at: soundblast@example.com
